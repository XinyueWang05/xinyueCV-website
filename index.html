Research & Teaching

Research

My research focuses on radiant energy budget, internal heat, and atmospheric dynamics of terrestrial and giant planets, as well as climate change, greenhouse and trace gases, wildfires, carbon cycle, and atmospheric energetics.

Research Interests

  • Planetary Science: radiant energy budget, internal heat, and atmospheric dynamics of terrestrial and giant planets
  • Atmospheric and Environmental Sciences: climate change, greenhouse/trace gases, wildfires, carbon cycle, and atmospheric energetics

研究方向

主要研究行星大气、行星系统辐射能量收支、巨行星内部热源以及大气动力学过程，同时关注地球系统科学中的碳循环与痕量气体问题。
